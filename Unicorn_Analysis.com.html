# Core cleaning function def clean_currency_to_int(value): if pd.isna(value): # handle NaN return np.nan val_str = str(value).replace('$', '').strip() try: if val_str.endswith('B'): return int(float(val_str[:-1]) * 1_000_000_000) elif val_str.endswith('M'): return int(float(val_str[:-1]) * 1_000_000) elif val_str.endswith('K'): return int(float(val_str[:-1]) * 1_000) except ValueError: return np.nan

12 — Conclusion

Key Takeaways

The unicorn landscape reflects a rapidly maturing global startup ecosystem — technology and financial services at its core, United States as its hub.

💼
Industry Dominance

Fintech & Internet Software dominate, accounting for ~40% of all unicorns globally combined. AI at 7.8% is the fastest growing sector.

🌎
USA Leads

The United States dominates with 52%+ of unicorns, far ahead of China (16%) and India (6%). San Francisco alone houses 152 companies.

📊
Right-Skewed Valuations

Valuations are heavily right-skewed — ~77% sit at $1B–$2B. Bytedance at $180B is an extreme outlier that inflates the mean significantly.

🚀
2021 Was Historic

414 new unicorns emerged in 2021, driven by post-COVID digital adoption, low interest rates, and a surge in tech investment globally.

🏦
Concentrated Investor Power

Sequoia, Tiger Global & Accel collectively back 250+ unicorn companies. US-based investors dominate with heavy cross-participation.

⚡
Speed to Unicorn

Median time is ~7 years, but companies like Candy.com and Flink Food achieved unicorn status in the same year they were founded (2021).
